### Logo

![Auguste Logo](../assets/auguste-logo.jpg)

The Auguste logo features:
- A **cookbook** symbolizing culinary knowledge and tradition
- A **spoon with circuit patterns** representing AI-powered intelligence
- The **Escoffier Green** background with **Champagne Gold** accents
- A circular badge design evoking quality and trust

### Primary Green: "Escoffier Green"
- **Hex:** `#1B3022`
- **Represents:** Fresh ingredients, sustainability, and the classic book cloth
- **Usage:** Primary backgrounds, headers, key UI elements

### Accent Gold: "Champagne Gold"  
- **Hex:** `#D4AF37`
- **Represents:** Quality, the precision of the AI, and the "Gold Standard" of culinary arts
- **Usage:** Accents, highlights, CTAs, premium features

### Typography

| Purpose | Font Family | Style |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Headings | **Playfair Display** | High-contrast Serif, elegant, classic |
| UI / Body | **Inter** | Clean, minimalist Sans-serif, highly readable |
